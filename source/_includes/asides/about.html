About Me

Who IS Navin R. Johnson?

I'm a very-soon-to-be father of one glorious chub of a baby boy. I use humor as a defense mechanism. I have a very hard time concentrating on one subject at a time. I own two chickens for their sweet, sweet eggs. And I named my cat Poopmouth.

I share my life with my wife who, in many ways, is my antithesis (for the better). We've staked our ground in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, in an old-ass house full of antiques. Somehow we survive it all.
